History and Founding

The Old Imperials Cricket Club represents the continuation of the Imperial College Union Cricket Club, and is made up of Imperial alumni. Founded in 2023, the alumni club has continued to grow and represents the best cricketers Imperial has ever produced.

With regular summer fixtures, including both Sunday games against local clubs, as well as weekly 8-a-side games in the Victoria Park Cricket League, we aim to provide competitive and enjoyable games, with a good atmosphere.

We also have regular socials, in the summer and the winter, an affiliated 5-a-side football team called Achton Villa, and an annual summer tour to an interesting country.

Our Journey

June 2023

Foundation

Old Imperials Cricket Club is established by some Imperial College alumni in the summer of 2023.

August 2023

First win

OICC establishes itself as one of the premier alumni cricket clubs in London, winning our first game against Hawks CC by 4 wickets.

2024

Expansion

In our first full season we play 6 games, winning 3.

November 2024

Granada Tour

The club embarks on its first overseas tour, entering the prestiguous "Granada Sixes" Tournament, finishing inside the top 6.

2025

Continued Growth

2025 sees us play another 7 games in London, winning 2. We also launch our first "Summer Smash" Tournament.

August 2025

Slovenia Tour

Our first summer tour sees us depart to Slovenia, where we win and lose one game each against Grosuplje CC.
