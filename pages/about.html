Heritage & Tradition

Old Imperials Cricket Club stands as a testament to the enduring spirit of Imperial College London's alumni community. Founded in 1924, our club has been bringing together generations of Imperials through a shared love of cricket.

More than just a cricket club, OICC represents the values instilled at Imperial College: scientific rigor, analytical thinking, innovation, and collaborative excellence. We embody the spirit of "Scientia" – knowledge – applying it both on and off the field.

Our members span multiple generations of Imperial alumni, from recent graduates to those who studied decades ago, all united by our common bond to the college and our passion for cricket.

Our Journey

1924

Foundation

Old Imperials Cricket Club established by Imperial College alumni committed to maintaining their connection to the college and the game of cricket.

1950s-1960s

Golden Era

OICC establishes itself as one of the premier alumni cricket clubs in London, competing regularly against Oxford and Cambridge alumni teams.

1980s-1990s

Expansion

The club expands its fixture list and welcomes a new generation of international Imperial alumni, reflecting the college's global reputation.

2023

Renaissance

A new committee revitalizes the club, bringing fresh energy while honoring traditions. Digital presence established and membership grows significantly.

2024-2026

Centenary Approach

As we approach our centenary, OICC continues to thrive with record membership, competitive fixtures, and a thriving social calendar. The tradition continues.
