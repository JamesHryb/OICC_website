How to Order

Kit is not sold on demand. Here's how the process works when an order window opens:

1

Watch for the announcement

The committee will post in the WhatsApp group and on this page when enough interest has been gathered for a bulk order.

2

Submit your items & sizes

You'll be asked to specify which items you want and your sizes. A sizing guide will be shared at that point.

3

Pay & wait for delivery

Payment is collected upfront. Shrey typically takes several weeks to produce and deliver bespoke kit.

All kit is manufactured and fulfilled by Shrey Sports. You can browse the full product range and see detailed product images on the official OICC club shop page.
